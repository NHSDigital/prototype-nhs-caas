About cohort enquirer

Cohort Enquirer is a self-service tool that allows you to search the NHS Data Processing Services (DPS) to determine if a patient is part of a cohort.

Cohort Enquirer will:

  • allow you to search for a patient by their NHS number and date of birth
  • show if a patient has been included or excluded in a cohort
  • help you to understand why a patient is part of a cohort
  • provide you with the correct information to answer cohort queries effectively

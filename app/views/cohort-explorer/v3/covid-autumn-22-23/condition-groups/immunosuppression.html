COVID-19 autumn booster vaccinations 2022 to 2023

Immunosuppression (COVID-19 2022 to 2023)

CaaS digital pathway rule logic

The table below shows the clinical rules used to determine who should be contacted or reminded to receive their COVID-19 autumn booster vaccinations based on specific medical conditions.

These conditions are detailed in the COVID-19 Green Book, chapter 14a.

Condition description Ruleset Datasets used
Condition description

People on immunosuppressive or immunomodulating therapy - first 3 months

 Ruleset

People over 5 years of age and recorded with any of the following within the last 3 months:

  • biologic immune modulators systemic medication
  • interleukin receptor inhibitors systemic medication
  • janus kinase inhibitors and other small-molecule inhibitors systemic medication
  • monoclonal tumour necrosis factor inhibitors systemic medication
  • soluble tumour necrosis factor receptors systemic medication
  • T-cell co-stimulation modulators systemic medication
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5
Condition description

People on immunosuppressive or immunomodulating therapy - first 6 months

 Ruleset

People who are:

  • over 5 years of age
  • recorded with B-cell depleting therapies systemic medication within the last 6 months
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5
Condition description

People prescribed steroids

 Ruleset

People who are:

  • over 5 years of age
  • recorded with COVID-19 corticosteroid medication 4 times on different days within the last 6 months
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5
Condition description

  • azathioprine

  • mycophenolate mofetil and mycophenolic sodium systemic medication
  • phosphoinositide 3-kinase inhibitor systemic medication
  • poly adenosine diphosphate-ribose polymerase inhibitors systemic medication
  • sirolimus systemic medication
  • sphingosine-1-phosphate receptor modulator systemic medication
  • steroid sparing systemic medication
  • tacrolimus systemic medication
  • tyrosine kinase inhibitor systemic medication
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5

  • radiotherapy

  • chemotherapy non-drug concept
  • chemotherapy systemic medication
  • radiotherapy treatment
  • recorded within SACT or RTDS datasets
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5, HES, RTDS, SACT

Conditions that may not be included in the CaaS digital pathway

If a person receives care from another healthcare provider, for example a hospital, specialist, or community clinic, their GP record is not automatically updated.

If a person’s GP record is not up-to-date when creating a cohort, they may not be included in the digital pathway.

This may result in a person not receiving the invite for their vaccination.

If you believe you're eligible for a vaccination, but have not received your invite, then visit the NHS website on the flu vaccine for more information.

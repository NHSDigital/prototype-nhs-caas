COVID-19 autumn booster vaccinations 2022 to 2023

Morbid obesity (COVID-19 2022 to 2023)

CaaS digital pathway rule logic

The table below shows the clinical rules used to determine who should be contacted or reminded to receive their COVID-19 autumn booster vaccinations based on specific medical conditions.

These conditions are detailed in the COVID-19 Green Book, chapter 14a.

Condition description Ruleset Datasets used
Condition description

People over 16 years of age with a body mass index (BMI) greater or equal to 40kg/m^2

 Ruleset

People who:

  • are over 16 years of age
  • have a BMI greater or equal to 40kg/m^2 recorded in the last 5 years
  • have no subsequent recorded BMI measurement lower than 40
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5, HES

Conditions that may not be included in the CaaS digital pathway

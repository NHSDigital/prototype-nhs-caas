Pregnancy (COVID-19 2022 to 2023)

COVID-19 vaccination in pregnancy

On the advice of NHS England, people who only qualify for a COVID-19 vaccination because they are pregnant are not included in the digital pathway.

In some areas of England, people who are pregnant can get their vaccination through antenatal clinics. In other regions, they will need an appointment at a GP surgery or a pharmacy offering the vaccination.

This applies to all people at any stage of their pregnancy (1st, 2nd or 3rd trimesters).

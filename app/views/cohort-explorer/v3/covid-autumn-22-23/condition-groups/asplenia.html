Asplenia or dysfunction of the spleen (COVID-19 2022 to 2023)

CaaS digital pathway rule logic

The table below shows the clinical rules used to determine who should be contacted or reminded to receive their COVID-19 autumn booster vaccinations based on specific medical conditions.

These conditions are detailed in the COVID-19 Green Book, chapter 14a.

Condition description Ruleset Datasets used
Condition description

People with asplenia or dysfunction of the spleen.

 Ruleset

People who are:

  • over 5 years of age
  • recorded with hereditary spherocytosis diagnosis
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5, HES
Condition description

People with asplenia; splenic dysfunction such as thalassemia major and celiac syndrome.

 Ruleset

People over 5 years of age and recorded with any of the following:

  • asplenia and splenic dysfunction
  • thalassaemia
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5, HES
Condition description

People with:

  • hereditary spherocytosis
  • homozygous sickle cell disease
 Ruleset

People who are:

  • over 5 years of age
  • recorded with sickle cell disease
 Datasets used GDPPR, GPES v4, GPES v5, HES

Conditions that may not be included in the CaaS digital pathway

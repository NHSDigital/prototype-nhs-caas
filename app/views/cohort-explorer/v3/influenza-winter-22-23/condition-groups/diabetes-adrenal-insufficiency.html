Influenza vaccinations 2022 to 2023

Diabetes and adrenal insufficiency

Digital pathway rule logic

The table below shows the digital cohort rules to determine who should be contacted or reminded to receive their flu vaccination based on specific medical conditions.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

    • Condition description

      People with:

      type 1 diabetes

      type 2 diabetes requiring insulin or oral hypoglycaemic drugs

      Addison’s disease

      secondary or tertiary adrenal insufficiency requiring steroid replacement

    • Ruleset

      People who are:

      over 6 months of age

      recorded with diabetes mellitus and not recovered since

    • Datasets used

      GPES v4 and GDPPR

Non-digital pathways

Some people identified in the clinical policy may not have been included in the digital pathway. This is because a complete medical record is not always held in a GP’s clinical system.

In this cohort, only diabetes conditions are included in the digital pathway.

People identified with Addison's disease on systemic steroids prescribed by their GP or hospital, may not be included in the digital cohort unless a GP practice uses a SNOMED CT code to show eligibility for flu vaccination.

If you believe you're eligible for a vaccination but have not been included in the digital cohort, visit the NHS website on the flu vaccine for more information.

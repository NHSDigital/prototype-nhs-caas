Conditions that may not be included in the CaaS digital pathway

If a person receives care from another healthcare provider, for example a hospital, specialist, or community clinic, the information stored in those local computer systems does not automatically update the records of their GP.

The GP record will need to be updated manually using any communication received from other healthcare providers.

This means that some people identified in the clinical policy may not have been included in the digital pathway.

If you believe you're eligible for a vaccination, but have not received your invite, then visit the NHS website on the flu vaccine for more information.

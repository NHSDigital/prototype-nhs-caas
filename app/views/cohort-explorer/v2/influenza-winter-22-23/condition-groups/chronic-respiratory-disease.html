Rule logic

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

    • Condition description

      Asthma that requires continuous or repeated use of inhaled steroids or systemic steroids.

    • Data used

      GPES v4 and GDPPR

    • Ruleset

      People who are:

      over 6 months of age

      recoded with asthma diagnosis and not recovered since

      and who are also:

      over 6 months of age

      recorded with asthma inhaled corticosteroids within the last 12 months

    • Condition description

      Asthma with previous exacerbations requiring hospital admission.

    • Data used

      GPES v4 and GDPPR

    • Ruleset

      People who are:

      over 6 months of age

      recorded with asthma emergency admissions between 1 September 202 and 31 March 2023

    • Condition description

      Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) including:

      chronic bronchitis and emphysema

      bronchiectasis

      cystic fibrosis

      interstitial lung fibrosis

      pneumoconiosis

      bronchopulmonary dysplasia (BPD)

    • Data used

      GPES v4 and GDPPR

    • Ruleset

      People who are:

      over 6 months of age

      recorded with chronic respiratory disease

Non-digital pathways

Data that is held within a GP clinical system does not hold a patients complete medical records. This means that a percentage of the patients identified within the clinical policy will not have been captured in the digital patient cohort.

If you believe you are entitled to an NHS flu vaccination and you have not been included in the digital cohort, you can visit the NHS website on the flu vaccine.

    • Condition description

      Asthma that requires continuous or repeated use of inhaled steroids or systemic steroids.

    • Conditions following a non-digital pathway

      The prescribing data for systemic steroids is not maintained centrally on GP records, so it’s not possible to determine the duration and dose of treatment.

      People identified as being on asthma treatment with systemic steroids, may not be included in the digital cohort unless a GP practice uses a SNOMED CT administrative code to show eligibility for flu vaccination.

    • Condition description

      Asthma with previous exacerbations requiring hospital admission.

    • Conditions following a non-digital pathway

      When it comes to data around hospital admissions, GP records, are not reliable.

      People identified based only on hospital admission data, may not be included in the digital cohort unless a GP practice uses a SNOMED CT code to show eligibility for flu vaccination.

    • Condition description

      Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) including:

      chronic bronchitis

      emphysema

      bronchiectasis

      cystic fibrosis

      interstitial lung fibrosis

      pneumoconiosis

      bronchopulmonary dysplasia (BPD)

    • Conditions following a non-digital pathway

      All conditions are included in digital pathway.

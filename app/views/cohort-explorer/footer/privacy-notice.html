Privacy notice

The CaaS service is run by NHS England. We want everyone to be able to use it.

If you access our service using your NHS Care Identity Service 2 details, the identity access and management services are managed by NHS Digital.

NHS Digital is the "controller" for any personal information you provided to NHS Digital to get a national digital identity via the NHS Care Identity Service 2 and authenticate your claim to that identity. It uses that personal information solely for that single purpose.

For any personal information, our role is the "processor" only and we must act under the instructions provided by NHS Digital (as the "controller") when verifying your identity.

Read NHS Digital's privacy notice and terms and conditions for the NHS Care Identity Service 2 (opens in new window).

This restriction does not apply to personal information you provide to us separately, which is managed in accordance with our privacy policy.

Rule logic

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

Condition description

GP added code to indicate vaccination eligibility for flu.

Ruleset

All people over 6 months with the SNOMED CT code 185903001 needs flu immunisation (finding) added to their GP record between 1 September 2022 to 31 March 2023.

All people over 6 months of age with the SNOMED CT code 935351000000106 long term indication for seasonal flu vaccination (situation) added to their GP record at any time.

Data used

GPES v4 and GDPPR

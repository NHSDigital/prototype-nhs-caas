Rule logic

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

Condition description

  • chronic kidney disease at stage 3, 4 or 5
  • chronic kidney failure
  • nephrotic syndrome
  • kidney transplantation

Ruleset

All people over 6 months of age with a diagnosis or history of:

  • chronic kidney disease stage 3, 4, 5 (where diagnosis has not been superseded by a CKD stage 1/2 diagnosis)
  • renal transplant, or dialysis for chronic kidney disease

Data used

GPES v4 and GDPPR

Non-digital pathways

All conditions are included in the digital pathway.

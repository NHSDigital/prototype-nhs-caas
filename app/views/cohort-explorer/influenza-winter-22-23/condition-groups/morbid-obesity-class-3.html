Rule logic

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

Condition description

Adults with a Body Mass Index (BMI) ≥40 kg/m².

Ruleset

People who are:

  • over 16 years
  • recorded with a BMI equal to or over 40 at any time between 1 September 2022 to 31 March 2023
  • not recorded with a BMI between 1 September 2022 to 31 March 2023, but their last recorded BMI within the last 3 years was equal to over 40

Data used

GPES v4 and GDPPR

Non-digital pathways

All conditions are included in the digital pathway.

Rule logic

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

Condition description

  • congenital heart disease with cardiac complications
  • chronic heart failure
  • individuals requiring regular medication and/or follow-up for ischaemic heart disease

Ruleset

All people over 6 months of age with a diagnosis of:

  • congenital heart disease
  • hypertension with cardiac complications
  • chronic heart failure or ischaemic heart disease (regardless of medication or hospital follow up

Data used

GPES v4 and GDPPR

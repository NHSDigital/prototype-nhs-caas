Rule logic

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

    • Condition description

      Conditions in which respiratory function may be compromised due to neurological disease (e.g. polio syndrome sufferers).

      Clinicians should offer immunisation, based on individual assessment, to clinically vulnerable individuals.

      This includes those with cerebral palsy, learning disabilities, multiple sclerosis and related or similar conditions; or hereditary and degenerative disease of the nervous system or muscles; or severe neurological disability.

    • Data used

      GPES v4 and GDPPR

    • Ruleset

      All people over 6 months of age diagnosed with a neurological condition where respiratory compromise is often severe and occult, such as Motor Neuron Disease, Parkinson’s Disease and Huntington’s Chorea.

    • Condition description

      Stroke, transient ischaemic attack (TIA)

    • Data used

      GPES v4 and GDPPR

    • Ruleset

      All people over 6 months of age diagnosed with stroke or TIA

Non-digital pathways

Data that is held within a GP clinical system does not hold a patients complete medical records. This means that a percentage of the patients identified within the clinical policy will not have been captured in the digital patient cohort.

The conditions below are not included in the digital cohort.

If you believe you are entitled to an NHS flu vaccination and you have not been included in the digital cohort, you can visit the NHS website on the influenza vaccine.

    • Condition description

      Conditions in which respiratory function may be compromised due to neurological disease (e.g. polio syndrome sufferers).

      Clinicians should offer immunisation, based on individual assessment, to clinically vulnerable individuals.

      This includes those with cerebral palsy, learning disabilities, multiple sclerosis, and related or similar conditions.

      Also included are those with hereditary and degenerative disease of the nervous system or muscles, or severe neurological disability.

    • Ruleset

      Eligibility for flu vaccination where respiratory compromise is variable (such as in multiple sclerosis and cerebral palsy), may not be included in the digital cohort unless a GP practice adds a SNOMED CT administrative code.

    • Condition description

      Stroke, TIA

    • Ruleset

      All conditions are included in digital pathway.

Rule logic

Pregnant people at any stage of pregnancy (1st, 2nd or 3rd trimesters).

The information below shows the digital pathway rule logic for the conditions that have been identified for call and recall for the flu season winter 2022 to 2023.

These conditions are detailed in chapter 19 of the influenza Green Book.

Condition description

Pregnant people at any stage of pregnancy (1st, 2nd or 3rd trimesters).

Ruleset

None.

Data used

GPES v4 and GDPPR

Information:

View the code lists for this condition group.

Non-digital pathways

Data that is held within a GP clinical system does not hold a patients complete medical records. This means that a percentage of the patients identified within the clinical policy will not have been captured in the digital patient cohort.

The conditions below are not included in the digital cohort.

If you believe you are entitled to an NHS flu vaccination and you have not been included in the digital cohort, you can visit the NHS website on the flu vaccine.

Clinical conditions

Pregnant people at any stage of pregnancy (1st, 2nd or 3rd trimesters).

Conditions following a non-digital pathway

On the advice of NHS England, pregnant people, where pregnancy is the only criteria with which they qualify for an flu vaccine, are not included in the digital pathway.

Pregnant people will be invited for flu vaccines through ante-natal clinics and GP practices.

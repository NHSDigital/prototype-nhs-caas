Our service

Cohorting as a Service helps to identify people that need direct care by creating population health cohorts (groups) based on specific health criteria.

Information:

Our service is currently only suitable for direct care. This is defined in the Information Governance Review as "a clinical, social or public health activity that involves the prevention, investigation and treatment of illness and the alleviation of suffering of an identified individual."

Building a cohort

You will build a cohort by adding:

  • the cohort name and purpose
  • the demographic rules
  • the clinically assured condition groups
  • the clinical rules

Submitting a cohort

When you have provided all the information on the cohort, you will be able to:

  • save a draft cohort
  • preview the cohort before publishing
  • submit the cohort for assurance

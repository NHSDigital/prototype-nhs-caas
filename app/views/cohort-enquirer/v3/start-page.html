Cohort Enquirer

You can use this self-service tool to understand why a patient is part of a cohort. It provides a patient's cohort history including the conditions that make them eligible.

What patient details you need

To search for a patient's cohort information, you will need both the patient's NHS number and date of birth

Information:

We can only provide patient information processed on or after September 2023.

If you need information from before September 2023, create a request through ServiceNow.

How to access

To access the tool, you must log in through the Care Identity Service (CIS2) and select the correct role.

Contact your Registration Authority if you do not have access to the CIS2 application.

By using this tool, you are agreeing to our terms and conditions and transparency notice. You are also agreeing to the terms of release for processing confidential patient information.

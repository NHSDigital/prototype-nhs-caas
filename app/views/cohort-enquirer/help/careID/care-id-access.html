How to get Care ID access

Care ID allows you to access patient data using a smartcard or other authentication methods.

To access this service, you will need to be invited by a Registration Authority or sponsor.

How to find your local Registration authority

Finding your local RA can vary depending on your organisation or department.

It may be part of:

  • an IT team
  • human resources
  • a completely seperate department

It may also be called something other than RA, for example, Smartcard Team.

If you do not know who your local RA is, you can:

  • ask a colleague
  • contact your IT helpdesk
  • check the primary care service provider contact details page to find out if your organisation is listed

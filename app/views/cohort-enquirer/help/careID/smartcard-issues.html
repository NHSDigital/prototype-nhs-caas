Help with your smartcard

If you are having difficulty authenticating with your smartcard, visit the smartcard and authenticator users webpage.

This webpage will give you help and support if you:

  • need your smartcard unlocked
  • have forgotten your passcode or want to change it
  • have to renew a smartcard certificate
  • lose your smartcard

Check that your device is configured correctly

You can check your smartcard set up to find out if your device is set up to work with Care Identity Authentication.

The tool will perform a check to detect whether the device's operating system and browser are supported.

Common issues and errors

Sometimes you may experience an error when trying to use your smartcard.

The smartcard troubleshooting webpage gives you help and guidance on:

  • understanding error codes
  • what to do if you get an error
  • how to contact support if the issue continues

By following the guidance, you should be able to resolve most smartcard issues.

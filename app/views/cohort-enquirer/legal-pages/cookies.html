Cookie policy

NHS England ("we" or "us") uses cookies to deliver the Find out why a patient is in a cohort tool and access to its services.

The information set out in this policy is provided in addition to the NHS England privacy policy and should be read alongside it.

What are cookies?

Cookies are widely used to make websites and apps work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide services and functionalities for users.

Cookies are not viruses or computer programs. They are very small so do not take up much space.

Visit www.aboutcookies.org for more information about cookies.

How we use cookies

We only use cookies to:

  • make our tool work (necessary cookies)/li>
  • measure how you use our website, such as which links you click on (analytics cookies), if you give us permission

We will ask for your consent before putting optional cookies on your device. Optional cookies will only be placed on your device when you request relevant added or alternative functionality.

Strictly necessary cookies

Necessary cookies enable core functionality such as page navigation and access to secure areas. The tool cannot function properly without these cookies.

List of necessary cookies that make our tool work
Name Purpose Expires
_state Remembers unique reference to your login attempt. Used as part of the OpenID Connect login flow to ensure security. 5 minutes after login attempt starts.

These cookies store information about how you use our tool, such as the links you click on.

Changes to cookies

Our cookie policy may change. The latest version of our cookie policy will be accessible through the tool. We will inform you through the tool if we make any material changes to our cookies policy. This will allow you to refresh your consent if you wish to continue using the tool.
